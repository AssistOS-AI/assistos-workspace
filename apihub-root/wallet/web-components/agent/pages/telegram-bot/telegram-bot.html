How to link your agent with Telegram:
  1. Open Telegram and search for BotFather
  2. Type /newbot
  3. Choose a name and a username for your bot(Use agent name).
  4. After that copy the bot ID and paste it here and click Start Bot
  5. Search your newly created bot in Telegram and press start. Then follow its instructions to link your identity to AssistOS
